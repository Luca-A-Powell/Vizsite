Hey there, I'm Luca. I'm a reporter at the Richmond Times-Dispatch. My specialty is accountability journalism. Over the years, I've written about stolen art in Virginia's museums, polluting plastics plants, state prosecutors burying evidence and plenty of state and local government exposes.

Before working at the Times-Dispatch, I covered Traverse City, Michigan, for the Traverse City Record-Eagle, where my project on guardianship abuses in northern Michigan was a finalist for the Scripps Award.

I'm a proud graduate of the CUNY Newmark Graduate School of Journalism.

This site is also a home for my data viz work. Thanks for stopping by.
